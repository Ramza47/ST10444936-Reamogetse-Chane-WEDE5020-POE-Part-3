Types of Services We Offer at ASG Logistics

At ASG, we utilize state-of-the-art drone technology to bring packages directly to our customers. Our drone delivery service offers a fast, safe, and environmentally friendly way to receive your purchases.

Our delivery products are imported from various countries.

Our goods and services are stored in a warehouse and ready to be distributed.

Why Choose Drone Delivery?

Our drones offer lightning-fast delivery, bypassing traditional roadways and traffic. With our real-time tracking system, you can monitor your package`s journey from the warehouse to your door. We deliver packages within minutes, ensuring convenience and efficiency.

Our drones in action, delivering products to customers.

Our drones are capable of carrying heavy products.

Equipped with high-tech cameras, our drones can reach various locations efficiently, ensuring customer satisfaction. Order from ASG Logistics and receive your parcel within a day!

How It Works

  1. Place Your Order: Choose your products and select the drone delivery option at checkout.
  2. Drone Dispatch: Once your order is prepared, the nearest drone is dispatched to your location. You will receive a notification when your package is loaded and en route.
  3. Receive Your Delivery: Our drones use precision landing technology to ensure safe, accurate drop-offs at your chosen location.
  4. Enjoy: Your package arrives quickly, ready for you to use or enjoy!

Safety and Regulations

Safety is our top priority at ASG Logistics. Our drones comply with local and federal aviation regulations and are equipped with advanced obstacle avoidance systems. Each drone has redundant safety features to ensure secure operations in all weather conditions.
